ABOUT

Enter the realm of Going Beyond Relationship, curated by Priscilla Eziwan—Kenya Certified Christian Marriage and Family Care counselor, content creator, Christian leader, and podcast host. Three pillars define our journey:
1. Understanding God's Will for Relationships: Priscilla, a beacon of wisdom, guides to uncover the divine blueprint for enduring and joy-filled connections by aligning with God's will.
2. Discovering God's Assignment and Purpose: Going Beyond relationships, embark on a profound journey of self-discovery. Priscilla encourages individuals to unearth their unique life assignment, fostering personal growth and connection to purpose.
3. Submission and Surrender to God's Will: Our platform spotlights the transformative power of submission. By entrusting relationships, marriages, and lives to God, we unlock doors to divine guidance and abundant blessings. At Going Beyond Relationship, we believe young hearts can make informed decisions. Through prayer, preparation, and a commitment to understanding God's will, we empower individuals to embark on a journey of fulfilling connections. Join us in this inspiring expedition where faith, relationships, and purpose converge. Priscilla Eziwan invites you to explore your potential and the beauty that awaits when you dare to go beyond. Welcome to a community where love, faith, and purpose intersect—Welcome to Going Beyond Relationship.
